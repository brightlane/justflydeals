Expert Advice

10 Tips to Find the Cheapest Flights

Strategies used by frequent flyers to cut airfare by 30–60%.

01
Book on Tuesday or WednesdayAirfare is cheapest mid-week. Avoid Friday–Sunday when prices spike 15–25%.
02
Fly early morning or late nightOff-peak departures are 20–30% cheaper with lower cancellation rates.
03
Be flexible with datesShifting your trip by 1–3 days can save $100–$400, especially around holidays.
04
Book domestic 6–8 weeks outThe sweet spot for US flights. Too early or too late costs more.
05
Book international 3–6 months outFor transatlantic and transpacific routes, book well in advance for best fares.
06
Try nearby airportsA secondary airport 60–90 minutes away can cut your fare by up to 40%.
07
Set fare alerts on JustFlyGet notified the instant prices drop on your route. Never miss a deal.
08
Search in incognito modeAirlines track repeat searches and raise prices. Always use a private window.
09
Bundle flight + hotelBooking together saves 15–40% over booking separately. Always check bundle pricing.
10
Consider a one-stop flightA connecting flight often costs 30–50% less than nonstop. Usually worth it.
